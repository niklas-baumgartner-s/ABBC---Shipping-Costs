Design - Our artists have many ideas for making stuff in clay! From delicate porcelain light sculptural work to heavy work and everything in between. Deciding on a product and its appearance is not easy, therefore starting with a design and trying everything will naturally gravitate to the style we and of course - YOU will enjoy. 

Making - As our artists work with clay, it first gets sloppier and wetter, before it begins to dry in the atmosphere. Considering the quality of our products and how we decorate our work, our process in "Making" can vary, such as whether we apply coloured slip in different level of clay wetness.

Drying - After the first two process (design and making) one of the most critical process is the drying. Before setting our wet products outside on a sunny weather or on a rack, we have to make sure that all of our ceramic products are perfect to avoid cracking as result, or any other damages.

Trimming (and Cleaning Up) - After our work dries up, we call this "greenware" and it is as it's most fragile. Our artists work with much care with fingers spreas as much as possible. We use different tools such as a piece of fly wire to rub the work to knife or metal kidney for dynamic treatment.

Glazing - Decorating our work with colour with three main options for surface treatment requires time - and fun! Depending on our planned design, we glaze our products in different ways - Oxide washes, Underglazes or Glazes!
